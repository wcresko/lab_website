RAD Sequencing Named ‘Area to Watch’ in Science’s Breakthrough of the Year

Genome scans and RAD sequencing have been named an “Area to Watch” in Science magazine’s prestigious Breakthrough of the Year issue!

This recognition by one of the world’s leading scientific journals validates the transformative impact of RAD-seq technology, which the Cresko Lab helped pioneer, on genomics research and its potential to revolutionize our understanding of evolution, ecology, and biodiversity.

Science highlights RAD-seq as: - Emerging technology to watch - Transformative genomics tool - Game-changer for evolution - Democratizing force in genomics - Future breakthrough candidate
