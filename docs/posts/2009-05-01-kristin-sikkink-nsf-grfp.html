Congratulations to Kristin Sikkink on receiving the highly competitive NSF Graduate Research Fellowship!

This prestigious award recognizes Kristin’s exceptional potential as a scientist and will provide three years of support for her doctoral research on evolutionary genomics and adaptation. The NSF GRFP is one of the most competitive graduate fellowships in the country.

The NSF GRFP represents: - National recognition - Exceptional achievement - Research potential - Academic excellence - Career launching award

Research Support

The fellowship provides: - Three years of funding - Stipend support - Research flexibility - Professional development - Conference travel

Kristin’s Research

Her work focuses on: - Evolutionary genomics - Adaptation mechanisms - Cryptic variation - Gene expression evolution - Environmental responses
